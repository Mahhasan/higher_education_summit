Our Mission

To develop academia-industry relationship & create direct proper communication between prospective learners and the universities.

To promote the prospects and to explore the opportunity of Education loan/Education banking for higher education at home or abroad.

To create learning and business opportunities for students, faculty, institution-owners and EdTech companies, and to create network among them.



Our Vision

104 private university in one place with financial organizations & education supporting industry.

Establishing a quality full admission process & provide valuable information, along with scholarships.

Create a complete eco-system of higher education seekers & providers (private) end to end to support youth.

University & Sponsors’ Benefits

Branding facilities start from registration to gate, banners and the print media.

Large number of College students’ and guardians’ engagement.

We will promote all the registered universities and sponsors in social media and digital marketing promotion.

Universities will get the opportunity of branding with some specific colleges in its vicinity.

Higher Education Seekers’ Benefits

Will find most of the universities, banks & IT Products in one place.

Getting easy Scholarship/ Waiver/Discount in admission.

Financial support and motivation through skill development seminars, Workshop & Proper counseling.

A proper idea about all the university education facilities & systems.

Supportive Programs

Waivers/ Scholarships/ Discounts in Admissions & on the spot selection, Psychometric Tests.

Each university will get a specific time-slot to show their activities, systems & achievements to connect to the students & guardian .

Showing different documentaries & glimpses of various university & other sponsors.

Closing programs

Prize giving ceremony for the best admission confirming universities, & some others.

Raffle draw, students fun & learn games, quiz competition, JAM Sessions.

One Concert & cultural performances from the universities interested.
